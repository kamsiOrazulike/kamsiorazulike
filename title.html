How to Play Humpty Hangman:
1. Determine the desired difficulty level quickly
2. Begin guessing letters by clicking on them using the mouse wisely
3. If the letter is within the word then Humpty remains sitting on the wall peacefully
4. If the letter does not belong in the word Humpty will lose balance gradually
5. Only when struggling, use a hint cautiously
6. 10 incorrect answers will cause the great fall, horrifically
7. The next word appears and the same applies to it, obviously
